## AI Supervision: Watchdog

The Watchdog system monitors conversations for policy violations. When a violation is detected, the conversation is automatically locked and the user is notified with clear warning banners. Configurable per-prompt by administrators.

<p align="center">
  <img src="watchdog-locked-conversation.png" alt="Watchdog Locked Conversation" width="800" />

  <em>Locked conversation after Watchdog detected policy violations, with warning banners displayed</em>
</p>
