Capabilities

Platform architecture

Enterprise-grade infrastructure designed for flexibility and scale.

Multi-Provider AI

Unified access to OpenAI, Anthropic, Google AI, and xAI. Switch providers without code changes. Bring your own API keys or use platform credits.

White-Label Architecture

Custom domains, branding, themes, and email templates. Deploy Aurvek as your own platform with complete visual customization.

Flexible Economics

Per-message pricing, subscriptions, or free tiers. Platform builders retain 70% of their markup. Full Stripe integration for payments.

Real-Time Streaming

WebSocket-based response delivery with streaming support across all integrated providers. Sub-second response initiation.

Voice Integration

Production-ready conversational voice AI powered by ElevenLabs. Natural voice interactions with transcript synchronization.

Security Built-In

JWT authentication, secure password storage, and role-based access controls. Your API keys stay encrypted.
