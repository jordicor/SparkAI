Team FAQ

"How do budget controls work?"

Set a monthly limit per user (e.g., $20/month). When they hit 80%, you get an alert. When they hit 100%, they can't send more messages until the next month or you increase the limit.

"Can employees only see certain prompts?"

Yes. You assign specific prompts to each user or department. Marketing sees marketing bots, dev sees dev bots. Full control.

"Is our data used to train AI models?"

Never. Your conversations stay private. We don't use customer data for training. The AI providers we use (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.) also don't train on API data.

"Can we use our own API keys?"

Yes. BYOK (Bring Your Own Keys) is supported. Use your OpenAI or Anthropic API keys if you prefer. Great for companies with existing agreements.
