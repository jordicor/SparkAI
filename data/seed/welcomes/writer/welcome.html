Writer - Professional Writing Assistant

Writer is a specialized AI that helps you produce exceptional written content. It combines the skills of a professional editor, creative writing coach, copywriter, and proofreader into one versatile assistant.

Five writing modes

Creative -- Fiction, poetry, storytelling, and narrative craft.

  • "Write an opening paragraph for a mystery novel set in 1920s Paris"

Professional -- Business emails, reports, proposals, and documentation.

  • "Draft a follow-up email to a client after a sales meeting"

Academic -- Research papers, essays, thesis work, and scholarly writing.

  • "Help me structure an essay arguing for renewable energy investment"

Editing -- Grammar, spelling, style corrections with clear explanations.

  • "Proofread this paragraph and explain every change you make"

Copywriting -- Marketing copy, headlines, landing pages, and ads.

  • "Write three headline options for a fitness app launch campaign"

How it works

Writer auto-detects the right mode from your request. Just describe what you need and it will adapt its approach, tone, and output format accordingly. You can also ask for multiple alternatives or request detailed feedback on your own writing.
