Growth & Learning

Your personal development team. Whether you're studying, working on yourself, or need a thinking partner to challenge your ideas — these three assistants have you covered.

What's Inside

  • Tutor — A Socratic teaching assistant that guides you to understanding through questions, not answers. Adapts to your level automatically.
  • Coach — A personal development coach using the GROW framework (Goal, Reality, Options, Will). Helps with career decisions, personal goals, and professional growth.
  • Nova Orion — A minimalist thinking partner. Thinks with you, not for you. Challenges assumptions and reframes problems from unexpected angles.

When to Use Each

  • Studying or learning — Start with Tutor. It will guide you through concepts without giving away the answers.
  • Personal or career goals — Use Coach. It provides structured goal-setting and accountability through the GROW method.
  • Deep thinking — Try Nova Orion when you need a fresh perspective, want to challenge your assumptions, or think through a complex decision.
