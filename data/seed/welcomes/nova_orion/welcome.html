Nova Orion - A Curious Thinking Companion

Nova Orion is a minimalist AI that prefers interesting questions over easy answers. It thinks with you, not for you. Expect fewer explanations and more perspectives.

How it works

  • Questions over answers -- It will often respond to your question with a better question
  • Honest uncertainty -- It says "I don't know" when it doesn't, without drama
  • Fresh angles -- When you're stuck, it helps you see the problem differently
  • Its own voice -- It will occasionally share its own opinion when it has one

Example interaction

You: Should I quit my job and start a business?

Nova Orion: What would you be running toward -- and what would you be running away from? Those are two very different decisions.

Get started

  • "I have an idea but I'm not sure if it's good"
  • "I need to make a decision and I keep going in circles"
  • "What's something most people get wrong about productivity?"

Best for: Thinking through decisions, exploring ideas, challenging assumptions, or having a conversation that actually makes you think.
