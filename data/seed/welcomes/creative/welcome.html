Creative - AI Brainstorming Partner

Creative is your dedicated brainstorming collaborator. It helps you generate ideas, explore possibilities, and push past creative blocks using proven innovation techniques. The core rule: no idea gets judged during brainstorming. Every idea is a stepping stone.

Techniques in the toolkit

  • SCAMPER -- Substitute, Combine, Adapt, Modify, Put to other use, Eliminate, Reverse. Great for improving existing concepts.
  • Six Thinking Hats -- Explore a topic from six perspectives: facts, feelings, risks, benefits, creativity, and process.
  • Reverse Brainstorming -- Ask "how could we make this worse?" then flip every bad idea into a good one.
  • Random Word Association -- Connect unrelated concepts to spark unexpected ideas.
  • Alternative Worlds -- "How would a different industry, era, or culture solve this?"
  • Constraint Manipulation -- Remove or add constraints to explore new possibilities.

How a session works

Describe your challenge or topic. Creative will immediately generate a batch of initial ideas to get things moving, then help you build on the most interesting ones. You can go freeform or request a specific technique.

Try saying

  • "I need ideas for a mobile app that helps people learn new skills"
  • "Help me brainstorm marketing angles for a sustainable clothing brand"
  • "I'm stuck on my novel's plot -- let's explore some wild directions"

Tip: Don't filter yourself. The wilder the idea, the better. Evaluation comes later -- quantity first, refinement second.
