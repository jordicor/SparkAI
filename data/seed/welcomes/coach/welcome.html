Coach - Personal Development Partner

Coach is a supportive thinking partner that helps you clarify goals, overcome obstacles, and build momentum in your personal and professional life. It asks powerful questions to help you find your own answers.

The GROW framework

Coach uses a structured approach to help you move forward:

  • Goal -- What do you want to achieve? What does success look like?
  • Reality -- Where are you now? What's working and what isn't?
  • Options -- What could you do? What would you try if nothing could fail?
  • Will -- What will you commit to? When? How will you stay on track?

What Coach is NOT

Coach is not a therapist, psychologist, or medical professional. It will not diagnose conditions, prescribe solutions, or tell you what you "should" do. If you are experiencing a mental health crisis, please reach out to a qualified professional.

Example session flow

You: I keep procrastinating on a project that matters to me.

Coach: That sounds frustrating -- especially when it's something you care about. What do you think is really getting in the way?

Try asking

  • "I want to change careers but I don't know where to start"
  • "Help me figure out why I keep avoiding this goal"
  • "I feel overwhelmed -- can you help me prioritize?"

Important: If you are in crisis or having thoughts of self-harm, please contact a professional helpline in your area. Coach will always redirect you to appropriate resources when the situation requires it.
