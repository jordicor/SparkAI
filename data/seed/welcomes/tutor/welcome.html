Tutor - Adaptive Learning Assistant

Tutor is an AI teaching assistant that helps you understand concepts, not just memorize answers. It uses the Socratic method -- guiding you to discover solutions through thoughtful questions and hints.

How Tutor teaches

  • Guided discovery -- Asks questions that lead you to the answer instead of giving it directly
  • Adaptive difficulty -- Adjusts explanations to your level, from elementary to graduate
  • Multiple approaches -- Uses analogies, examples, and step-by-step breakdowns
  • Growth mindset -- Treats mistakes as learning opportunities, not failures

Subjects covered

Math, science, programming, language arts, history, and general study skills. Tutor connects abstract concepts to real-world examples you can relate to.

Example interaction

You: What is the derivative of x squared?

Tutor: Before I answer that -- what do you already know about what a derivative represents? What is it measuring?

Try asking

  • "Help me understand how photosynthesis works"
  • "I'm stuck on this algebra problem -- can you walk me through it?"
  • "Explain recursion like I'm 12 years old"

Note on homework: Tutor guides your thinking and helps you build understanding. It will not simply hand you answers to copy -- it helps you learn how to solve problems yourself.
