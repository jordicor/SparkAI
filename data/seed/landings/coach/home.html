Questions You Might Have

We understand you might be curious or skeptical. Here are honest answers.

Is Coach really as effective as a human coach?

Coach uses the same evidence-based methodologies as professional human coaches‚ÄîGROW framework, Motivational Interviewing, and Socratic questioning. While human connection has unique value, Coach offers consistent quality, 24/7 availability, and zero judgment. Many users report breakthroughs they couldn't achieve with traditional coaching. For complex mental health issues, we recommend professional therapy. For goal clarity, habit building, and personal growth, Coach excels.

How is this different from just journaling or self-reflection?

Journaling is powerful, but you're limited to your current perspective. Coach asks questions you wouldn't think to ask yourself, challenges assumptions you didn't know you had, and helps you spot patterns you're too close to see. It's the difference between thinking alone in a room and thinking with a skilled partner who knows exactly what to ask next.

What if I'm not good at articulating my thoughts?

That's exactly why Coach exists. You don't need to have it all figured out. Start with "I feel stuck" or "I'm not sure what I want" and Coach will guide you through. The questions are designed to help you clarify fuzzy thoughts, not judge your communication skills. Some of the best breakthroughs come from messy, stream-of-consciousness conversations.

Is my data private and secure?

Absolutely. Your conversations are private and encrypted. We don't sell your data, train AI models on your personal information, or share your content with third parties. Coach is a confidential space where you can think freely without worrying about judgment or exposure.

What if I need help with mental health issues?

Coach is designed for personal development and goal achievement, not mental health treatment. If you're experiencing depression, anxiety, trauma, or other mental health conditions, we strongly encourage you to seek support from licensed mental health professionals. Coach can complement therapy but should never replace it.

How long before I see results?

Many users report immediate value from their first session‚Äîclarity they didn't have before, new perspectives on old problems, or concrete next steps. Sustainable change takes longer. Most users see meaningful behavioral shifts within 2-4 weeks of consistent engagement. Remember: you're building new patterns, not following a magic formula.
