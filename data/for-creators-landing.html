Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

"I'm not technical. Can I still do this?"

Absolutely. Our prompt builder guides you step-by-step. No coding required. If you can describe what your AI should do, you can create it.

"Aren't prompts becoming commodities?"

Generic prompts, yes. But you're not selling a prompt - you're selling a branded AI experience with your landing page, your domain, and your relationship with users. That's a business, not a commodity.

"Can't people just use ChatGPT directly?"

They can, but they won't get your expertise, your personality, your specific use case optimization. You're not competing with ChatGPT - you're building something unique on top of it.

"What about API costs?"

We handle all API costs. You set your price per message, users pay, and you get 70% of your markup. The base API cost is covered in our platform fee.

"How do I get users?"

Share your landing page link anywhere: social media, your website, email list, podcasts. Your page is SEO-optimized for organic discovery too.

"When can I withdraw earnings?"

Anytime after reaching $50 in pending earnings. We pay via Stripe directly to your bank account. No waiting for monthly cycles.
